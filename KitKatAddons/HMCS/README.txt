To install the HMD navigate to "SaccFlightAndVehicles/KitKatAddons/KitKatHMCS" and locate the prefab named "KitKatHMCSController".
Drag and drop this prefab into the "InVehicleOnly" of your SaccFlight vehicle of choice.

That's it.
If you wish to customize the angles at which the HUD disables you can just reference the x and y numbers at the bottom of the inspector in play mode when looking around.
